About these forecasts

Forecast maps show the expected magnitude of each parameter accumulated (rainfall, runoff) or averaged (soil moisture) over the next 7, 15 and 30 days, and the forecast Combined Drought Index for drought. Regional figures are area summaries over North, Northwest, Northeast, East, Central, West and South India. Forecasts are driven by the IMD Extended Range Forecast System.

The India Drought Monitor is developed by the Water and Climate Lab at the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar, drawing on hydro-meteorological data from the India Meteorological Department and the Central Water Commission, satellite products from ISRO/NRSC, and global datasets from NASA.
