{\large\bfseries\color{titleblue}Summary}\\[3pt]
{\justifying\normalsize\color{textblack}The national drought situation this week shows a distribution across the drought classes, with 80.4\% of the area in the Normal category and 19.6\% in the D0+ category. The total area affected by drought, specifically D0+, was 19.6\% this week, which represents a contraction of 0.5 percentage points compared to the previous week's 20.1\%. Looking further back, the area in D0+ was 15.0\% approximately one month ago. \par\medskip Gujarat exhibited the highest level of drought impact, with 85.2\% of its area in drought. Other states with significant drought presence include the NCT of Delhi, which had 69.6\% in drought, Arunanchal Pradesh at 52.8\%, Jammu \& Kashmir at 50.4\%, Mizoram at 49.8\%, and Meghalaya at 43.5\%. Conversely, Sikkim reported 0.0\% in drought, and Nagaland reported 0.7\% in drought.\par}
