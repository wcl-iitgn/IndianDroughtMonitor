{\large\bfseries\color{titleblue}Summary}\\[3pt]
{\justifying\normalsize\color{textblack}The national drought situation this week shows that the majority of the area remains in the Normal category, with 79.9\% of India in Normal conditions. \par\medskip The total drought area, classified as D0 or worse, increased to 20.1\% this week, representing an expansion of 4.8 percentage points compared to the previous week's 15.3\%. \par\medskip The states most affected by drought include Gujarat with 98.3\% of its area in drought, followed by the NCT of Delhi at 52.2\%, Maharashtra at 51.3\%, Jammu \& Kashmir at 47.9\%, Mizoram at 47.4\%, and Arunachal Pradesh at 46.6\%. Conversely, Sikkim, Nagaland, Tripura, and West Bengal reported 0.0\% of their area in drought.\par}
