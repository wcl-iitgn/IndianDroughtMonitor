# India Drought Monitor — National Summary for week ending 2026-05-20

The national drought situation this week is characterized by a large area remaining in the Normal category, with a small portion experiencing drought conditions. The total area affected by drought, specifically D0 or worse, was 15.3% this week. This figure shows a contraction compared to the previous week's 16.2% and the area observed approximately one month ago at 17.2%, indicating a decrease of 0.9 percentage points week-on-week. The regions most affected by drought conditions include NCT of Delhi, Manipur, Mizoram, Arunanchal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, and Meghalaya. Conversely, West Bengal recorded 0.0% in drought, and Rajasthan, Gujarat, and Telangana were noted as the least affected states with drought percentages of 1.0%, 2.1%, and 3.4%, respectively.
