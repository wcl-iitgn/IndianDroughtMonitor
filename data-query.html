Query the Data

Run your own read-only SQL (AlaSQL dialect) against the same in-browser tables that power the assistant: the national weekly drought series, the latest per-state breakdown, and the monthly Hydrological Outlook means. Everything runs locally in your browser — nothing is sent to a server. Only SELECT queries are allowed.

Results will appear here. Try one of the example queries on the right, or write your own.
